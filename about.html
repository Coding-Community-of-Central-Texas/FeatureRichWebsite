ABOUT OSCCCT

MISSION STATMENT

Our purpose is to unite like-minded individuals within our student organization, fostering an environment where we organize, discuss, and develop software. By creating a dynamic center for pursuing software-based projects, we aim to sharpen each other's programming skills while concurrently facilitating networking opportunities with both companies and individuals.
