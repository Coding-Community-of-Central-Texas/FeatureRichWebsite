Welcome to The OSCCCT Webpage

We are a vibrant student organization rooted at Texas A&M University - Central Texas, established in February 2024. Our journey began with the invaluable support of TAMUCT, renowned for its exceptional faculty and supportive environment. Comprising diligent, seasoned, and diverse individuals, every member of OSCCCT embodies the qualities emblematic of TAMUCT's ethos. Together, we epitomize dedication, expertise, and individuality, fostering a dynamic community poised for success.
